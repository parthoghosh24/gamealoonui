WHAT IS GAMEALOON?
What started as a personal video game blog in mid- 2012... and died down, turned up into a whole new thing in 2013. Gamealoon is probably world's first gamer sourced video game entertainment and media system which provides gamer generated reviews, features , news, gloonicles and videos about video games. What this mean that gamers get to share their experience, opinions and point of view in a professional way just like how articles are created in many gaming entertainment websites and that way, the world gets to know what people actually think about video games and Gaming industry in general.That means if a game gets 9.7/10, that is what the gamers think too thanks to gameloon's custom made game scoring algorithm. Also, gamealoon features a fully functional social network which allows gamers to create their network, interact and find out what their fellow gaming peers are doing. So what are you waiting for, register, login and go crazy over gaming. Welcome to Gamealoon.
